Odoo 10 Daily Invoice Report

This module shows the daily invoices, user know that how much amount he need to received from customers according to their sale team


Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo community edition but not tested in Enterprise version. And we are supporting Ubuntu OS and not Windows OS.

How to use

  • You can see the available Custom Reports menu for Daily Invoice Report.


  • The Daily Invoice opens a wizard with filters like Date, Sale Team and Partners.


  • Report will show summary of team and group by team and shows the total invoice amount of customer.
